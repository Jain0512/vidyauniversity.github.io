Department of Sports


The Department of Sports, under the Faculty of Sports & Education, is committed to fostering excellence in sports, physical education, and athletic development. The department provides students with scientific training methodologies, sports management skills, and physical fitness education, preparing them for careers in professional sports, coaching, fitness training, and sports science research. With a strong emphasis on sports training, biomechanics, nutrition, and psychology, the department aims to develop athletes, coaches, and sports professionals who can excel at national and international levels.
