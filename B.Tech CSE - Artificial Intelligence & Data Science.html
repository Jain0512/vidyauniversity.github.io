B.Tech CSE - Artificial Intelligence & Data Science


The CSE program specializing in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science provides students with a strong foundation in both AI techniques and data-driven problem-solving. Students learn key areas such as machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and data mining, which are essential for creating intelligent systems. The program emphasizes the use of statistical methods, data analysis, and data visualization tools to interpret large datasets. It also covers algorithm design, neural networks, and AI ethics. Students gain hands-on experience through practical projects, working with real-world datasets and AI frameworks. Elective courses allow for deeper exploration of specific topics like computer vision, robotics, and big data technologies. The curriculum integrates programming languages such as Python, R, and SQL for efficient data manipulation and model development. Graduates are equipped to work as data scientists, AI researchers, or machine learning engineers, solving complex problems across industries.
