MBA (Marketing)


The Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Marketing at Vidya University is a specialized two-year program designed for individuals aspiring to excel in the competitive world of marketing. The program blends core business management principles with advanced marketing strategies to prepare students for leadership roles in the dynamic and ever-evolving marketing industry. With a focus on digital transformation, consumer behaviour, branding, and global marketing trends, students will acquire the knowledge and skills needed to create impactful marketing strategies and drive business growth. This program emphasizes practical application, strategic decision-making, and innovation, equipping students to manage marketing functions at the highest levels.
