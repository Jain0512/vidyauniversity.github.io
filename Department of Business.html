Department of Business


The Department of Business, also known as the Department of Business Administration, is a crucial part of any organization or educational institution. Its primary focus is on providing education and training in business management, administration, and related fields.

Key Functions:

- Education and Research: Offers various programs, such as MBA, Executive MBA, and PhD, to develop business leaders and entrepreneurs.
- Business Development: Provides training and consulting services to businesses, entrepreneurs, and organizations.
- Policy Formulation: Contributes to the development of business policies and regulations.

Departments within a Business Organization:

- Finance Department: Oversees financial operations, including budgeting, forecasting, and financial reporting.
- Marketing Department: Develops and implements marketing strategies to promote products or services.
- Human Resources Department: Manages employee recruitment, training, and development.

Academic Institutions:

- Vidya University: Offers various business programs, including MBA, MBA (International Business), and PhD.
- University College of Commerce and Business Management: Provides postgraduate programs in Business Management.
These departments and institutions play a vital role in shaping business leaders, entrepreneurs, and policies that drive economic growth and development.
