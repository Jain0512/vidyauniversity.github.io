BBA LLB


The BBA LLB is a five-year integrated undergraduate program that combines business administration and law, offering students a comprehensive understanding of both fields. The curriculum is designed to equip students with knowledge in business management, economics, finance, and marketing alongside core legal subjects such as constitutional law, criminal law, corporate law, and intellectual property law. This dual approach allows graduates to pursue careers in law firms, corporations, government agencies, or even start their own ventures. The course also emphasizes practical learning through internships, moot courts, and legal research. Students gain crucial skills in critical thinking, negotiation, and leadership, making them versatile professionals. BBA LLB is ideal for those seeking to bridge the gap between business and law in their future careers.
