B.Tech CSE - Cyber Security


The CSE Cybersecurity program focuses on equipping students with the skills and knowledge necessary to protect digital systems, networks, and data from cyber threats. Students study a range of topics including network security, cryptography, ethical hacking, digital forensics, and security protocols. The program delves into risk management, security architecture, and incident response, ensuring students can prevent, detect, and respond to security breaches effectively. Practical, hands-on experience is gained through labs, simulations, and real-world case studies. Students also learn to navigate the legal and ethical aspects of cybersecurity, preparing them for roles in safeguarding sensitive information. Elective courses cover advanced topics like blockchain security, cloud security, and malware analysis. The program emphasizes a multidisciplinary approach, integrating knowledge of computer science, engineering, and security practices. Graduates are prepared for careers in cybersecurity, working as security analysts, penetration testers, or systems architects.
