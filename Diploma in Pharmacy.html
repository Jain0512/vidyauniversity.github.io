Diploma in Pharmacy


Diploma in Pharmacy (D. Pharm) is a two-year diploma program designed to provide students with a solid foundation in pharmaceutical sciences. This program serves as an entry point into the field of pharmacy, focusing on the study of drugs, their formulation, dispensing, and proper usage. It prepares students with essential knowledge and practical skills to become proficient in managing medications and contributing to the healthcare system. The D.Pharm program prepares students for rewarding careers as government pharmacists, in pharmaceutical industries, community pharmacist, hospitals, and more, ensuring their role in promoting health and well-being.
