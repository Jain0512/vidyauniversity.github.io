Master of Arts (MA) in Event Management is a comprehensive undergraduate program designed to prepare students for a successful career in the dynamic and fast-paced world of event planning and execution. This program combines creativity, management, and organizational skills to equip students with the knowledge and expertise required to plan, coordinate, and manage a wide range of events across various sectors, including corporate, entertainment, weddings, conferences, festivals, and more. The BA in Event Management offers a blend of theoretical knowledge, hands-on experience, and practical industry exposure to ensure students are well-prepared to thrive in the event industry. Students will learn how to manage all aspects of event planning, including budgeting, marketing, logistics, client relations, vendor management, and post-event evaluation.
