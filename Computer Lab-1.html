Computer Lab-1


Computer Lab 1 is a state-of-the-art facility exclusively dedicated to the students of the Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) department. The lab is designed to provide a hands-on learning environment with modern computing resources, high-speed internet, and the latest software tools to support programming, data analysis, AI/ML, and software development.

Lab Features & Infrastructure

✅ High-Performance Computers with the latest processors and ample RAM for seamless computing.
✅ High-Speed Internet & LAN Connectivity to facilitate research and cloud-based applications.
✅ Pre-installed Software & Tools, including:

Programming Languages: Python, Java, C, C++
Database Management: MySQL, PostgreSQL, MongoDB
Development Tools: Eclipse, Visual Studio Code, IntelliJ IDEA
AI & ML Frameworks: TensorFlow, PyTorch, OpenCV
Web Development: Node.js, React, Angular, Django
Cybersecurity Tools: Wireshark, Kali Linux, Metasploit

Purpose & Usage

Regular Lab Sessions: Supports core subjects like Data Structures, Algorithms, Operating Systems, DBMS, AI & ML, and Web Development.
Project Work & Research: Enables students to work on academic and industry-oriented projects.
Coding & Hackathons: Used for competitive programming, coding contests, and hackathons.
Workshops & Certifications: Conducts hands-on workshops on Cloud Computing, DevOps, and Cybersecurity. Guidelines & Access Open for CSE students during academic hours and extended timings for project work. Supervised by faculty members and lab assistants for guidance. Strict adherence to lab rules, including proper usage of equipment and ethical computing practices.
