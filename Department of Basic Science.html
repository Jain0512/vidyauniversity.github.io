Department of Basic Science


The Department of Basic Science is a fundamental part of any educational institution, research organization, or university. Its primary focus is on providing education, research, and training in the fundamental sciences, which include:

Disciplines

1. Physics: Studies the fundamental laws of nature, matter, energy, and the universe.
2. Chemistry: Examines the composition, properties, and reactions of matter.
3. Mathematics: Develops and applies mathematical theories, models, and techniques to solve problems.
4. Biology: Explores the structure, function, growth, evolution, and distribution of living organisms.
