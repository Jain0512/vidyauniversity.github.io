B.Tech in Electronics Engineering


The B.Tech in ECE program focuses on communication systems and embedded technology. Students study digital electronics, microprocessors, and signal processing. The curriculum includes IoT, VLSI design, and wireless communication. Industry collaborations provide exposure to real-world electronic applications. Practical labs enhance hands-on expertise in circuit design. The program prepares students for careers in telecommunications and automation. Research opportunities in AI and robotics expand career possibilities. Graduates can work in semiconductor industries, aerospace, or IT sectors. Advanced lab facilities provide practical experience in electronic system design. The course integrates modern engineering concepts with practical applications.
