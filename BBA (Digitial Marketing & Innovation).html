BBA (Digitial Marketing & Innovation)


The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Digital Marketing and Innovation at Vidya University is a specialized undergraduate program designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to thrive in the ever-evolving world of digital marketing and technological innovation. This three-year program integrates core business management principles with a strong emphasis on digital marketing strategies, emerging technologies, and innovative business solutions. The program combines theoretical learning with hands-on experience, preparing students for leadership roles where they can drive digital transformation, create innovative marketing campaigns, and harness the power of data and technology to enhance business success.
