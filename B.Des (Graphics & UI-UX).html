B.Des (Graphics & UI-UX)


The Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Graphics and UI-UX Design is an interdisciplinary program that focuses on the art and science of creating intuitive, visually engaging, and user-centric digital experiences. This program equips students with the technical skills and creative knowledge required to design digital interfaces and user experiences for a variety of platforms, from websites to mobile applications.
The curriculum blends graphic design fundamentals with the principles of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, ensuring that students graduate with a well-rounded understanding of how to craft seamless, aesthetically pleasing, and functional designs.

Career Opportunities


1. Graphic Designer: Create visual concepts for a range of media, including print and digital.
2. UI/UX Designer: Design user-friendly interfaces for websites and apps.
3. Web Designer: Create and maintain websites and web applications.
4. Illustrator: Create illustrations for books, magazines, and other media.
5. Art Director: Oversee the creative direction of design projects.
