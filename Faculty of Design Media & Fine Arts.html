Faculty of Design Media & Fine Arts


Vidya University's Faculty of Design, Media & Fine Arts is a hub for creative expression and intellectual exploration. We offer a diverse range of programs that cater to the aspirations of aspiring artists, designers, and thinkers. Our undergraduate and postgraduate programs in design, liberal arts, and fine arts are designed to cultivate creativity, critical thinking, and technical skills. The faculty's curriculum emphasizes a balance of theory and practice. Students benefit from hands-on projects, workshops, and industry collaborations, preparing them for the dynamic creative industries. Our dedicated faculty members, who are accomplished artists and designers, bring real-world experience to the classroom, inspiring students to push the boundaries of their creativity.
