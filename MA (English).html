MA (English)


The MA in English is a graduate program that focuses on deepening students' understanding of English literature, linguistics, and language theory. The curriculum covers diverse literary periods, genres, and critical approaches, helping students develop strong analytical and communication skills. In addition to literature, the program also emphasizes research methodology, creative writing, and language acquisition. Students gain the ability to critically evaluate texts, conduct independent research, and enhance their writing capabilities, which prepares them for careers in education, writing, publishing, and media.
