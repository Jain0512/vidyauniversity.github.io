Prof. BP Sharma Central Library


The BP Sharma Central Library at Vidya University is a hub of knowledge, learning, and research, catering to students, faculty, and researchers across various disciplines. With a vast collection of books, digital resources, and advanced research facilities, the library serves as a cornerstone of academic excellence.
Features & Facilities:
1. Extensive Collection Over 1 Lakh books, journals, and periodicals covering diverse fields such as Science, Technology, Management, Humanities, and Social Sciences.
2. Library Membership & Services Open to students, faculty, alumni, and researchers. Book lending services, interlibrary loan facility, and digital resource access for members.

It features spacious reading areas, computer terminals with internet access, and dedicated sections for group discussions and individual study. Modern amenities such as Wi-Fi connectivity, photocopying services, and a digital catalog system are available to facilitate easy access to resources. The library is equipped with an automated circulation system, allowing for efficient lending and returning of books.
