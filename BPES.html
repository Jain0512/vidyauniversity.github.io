After completing Bachelor of Physical Education and Sport Science (BPES) course, candidates can find employment opportunities as physical education instructor, professional player, sports administration, sports journalist, sports photojournalist, umpire/referee, trainer/ instructor/ coach/ therapists and sports industry
Bachelor of Physical Education & Sports (B.P.E.S) the course empowers student to acquire essential skill which will define him as a professional technical expert at the grass root level.
