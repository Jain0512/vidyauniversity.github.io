M.Sc. in Pharmaceutical Science


Pharmaceutical Chemistry is the study of drugs and drug development. This discipline includes concepts and processes of drug discovery, delivery, absorption, metabolism, elements of Biomedical Analysis, Pharmacology, Pharmacokinetics and Pharmacodynamics.
Postgraduates of pharmaceutical chemistry can be hired in different capacities such as Quality Control & Quality Assurance Analyst, Scientist, Researcher, Research Executive, Scientific Data Entry Specialist, Pharmaceutical Patent Analyst, etc.
The MSc in Pharmaceutical Chemistry is specifically designed for graduates with a c hemistry-based degree to tailor their expertise towards jobs in the pharmaceutical industry. The pharmaceutical industry is a global industry so the award is particularly attractive for overseas graduates looking to upskill to secure jobs the world over.
