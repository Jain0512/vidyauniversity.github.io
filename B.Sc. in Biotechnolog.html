B.Sc. in Biotechnolog


Biotechnology is a dynamic field that combines biology and technology to create innovative solutions for sectors like healthcare, agriculture, and environmental sustainability. By studying the molecular and cellular processes of living organisms, biotechnology enables the development of technologies that improve human health and the environment. Over 250 biotechnological products, including life-saving medicines, vaccines, and medical treatments, have been introduced globally, revolutionizing the way untreatable diseases are managed.
The B.Sc. Biotechnology program at VIDYA University spans 4 years and provides students with the knowledge to apply biological processes in industrial settings, focusing on areas such as microbiology, genetic engineering, and bioprocessing. The curriculum is designed to bridge biology and technology, and students are also encouraged to participate in seminars, workshops, and guest lectures that enhance their learning. With a wide range of career opportunities available across various industries, this program ensures graduates are well-equipped for roles in biotechnology, healthcare, research, and beyond.
