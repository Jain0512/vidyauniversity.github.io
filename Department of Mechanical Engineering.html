Department of Mechanical Engineering


The Department of Mechanical Engineering is dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in mechanical systems and engineering technologies. Our mission is to equip students with strong analytical skills, problem-solving abilities, and hands-on experience in the fields of design, manufacturing, thermodynamics, robotics, and automotive engineering. With a curriculum that blends theory and practical applications, we prepare students for diverse careers in aerospace, automotive, energy, robotics, and industrial automation.
