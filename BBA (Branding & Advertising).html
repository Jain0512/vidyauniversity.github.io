BBA (Branding & Advertising)


The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Branding and Advertising at Vidya University is a specialized undergraduate program designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the dynamic world of branding, advertising, and marketing communications. This three-year program integrates core business management principles with specialized knowledge of brand strategy, advertising techniques, and digital marketing. The program combines theoretical learning with hands-on experience to prepare students for leadership roles in the advertising and branding sectors, where they can create impactful, innovative campaigns that resonate with audiences and drive business success.
