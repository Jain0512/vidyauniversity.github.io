MBA (Business Analytics & AI)


The Master of Business Administration (MBA) in Business Analytics and AI at Vidya University is a two-year specialized program designed for individuals seeking to excel in the intersection of business management, data analytics, and artificial intelligence. The program integrates core business principles with cutting-edge analytics and AI technologies to prepare students for leadership roles in industries driven by data. Students will develop the skills required to harness big data, build AI-driven models, and use predictive analytics to drive informed decision-making and business transformation. This program emphasizes practical application, strategic problem-solving, and data-driven leadership, equipping students to lead in the age of digital transformation.
