Faculty of Commerce & Business


Vidya University's Faculty of Commerce and Business stands as a pillar for cultivating future business leaders. It offers a comprehensive suite of programs, catering to students at various stages of their academic journey, including undergraduate B. Com and BBA programs, postgraduate MBA programs, and a doctoral program. The faculty's curriculum prioritizes a well-rounded approach, seamlessly blending theoretical knowledge with practical applications. Students gain invaluable hands-on experience through case studies, projects, and industry collaborations, preparing them for the dynamic realities of the business world.
