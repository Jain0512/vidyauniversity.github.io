Faculty of Pharmacy


The Faculty of Pharmacy at Vidya University is committed to providing world-class education, cutting-edge research, and hands-on experience to students aspiring to pursue careers in the pharmaceutical and healthcare industries. With a strong emphasis on both theoretical knowledge and practical application, our program is designed to equip students with the necessary skills to excel in the dynamic and ever-evolving field of pharmacy. The Faculty of Pharmacy at Vidya University is your gateway to a fulfilling and impactful career in the pharmaceutical sciences. Whether you aim to become a researcher, a pharmacist, or an industry expert, our comprehensive programs and industry partnerships provide the foundation for your success in the pharmaceutical world.
