BBA (Sports Management)


The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Sports Management at Vidya University is a specialized undergraduate program designed to prepare students for leadership roles in the sports industry. This three-year program blends core business management principles with specialized knowledge of sports operations, marketing, and event management. By combining theoretical learning with practical exposure, the program equips students with the skills necessary to manage sports teams, events, and organizations effectively. Students will also gain insights into sports economics, media, and sponsorship, positioning them for impactful careers in the dynamic world of sports management.
