Suvarna Girls Hostel


For the security & safety of the female candidates, the girls Hostel Suvarna is centrally located in the campus with 150 rooms and a capacity to accommodate 300 students on twin sharing basis. Away from the noise and the chaos of the city the hostel provides the safest environment for the female candidates. The hostel has its own dining hall facility which serves good and nutritious food. The management of the hostel is under Dean Students Affairs, Chief Warden, and Wardens. The hostel has an impeccable security system to ensure the safety of your girl child. The VKP campus hosts a separate accommodation for boys and girls in twin sharing formats. The rooms are spacious, hygienic, well ventilated and fully furnished for residents. The hostels are well equipped with 24 hours electricity, water supply, water coolers, recreational facilities including T.V. and indoor games such as table tennis, gymnasium, chess, carom and lot more. Wi-Fi Enabled hostel premises are fully secured with CCTV cameras and round the clock security. The campus also has medical assistance with a 24Ã—7-ambulance facility. The Food is an essential part of the hostel stay and we have a dedicated Hostel Mess in each of the hostels, which are equipped with modern appliances and serves pure vegetarian, nutritious and hygienic food. Each Hostel is managed by a loving, caring and protective warden who resides in the hostel campus and look after the day to day needs of the students. Our deep concern along with the regular mentoring ensure a safe and happy living away from home.
