BBA (Business Analytics)


The Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Business Analytics at Vidya University is a specialized undergraduate program designed to prepare students for leadership roles in the data-driven business world. This three-year program integrates core business management principles with cutting-edge business analytics techniques and technologies. Students will learn how to leverage data and analytics tools to make informed business decisions, optimize operations, and drive strategic growth. Combining theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience, this program equips students to solve complex business problems using data, statistical methods, and analytical tools.

Career Opportunities


Graduates with this degree can pursue careers in data analysis, market research, financial analytics, and strategic planning across various industries such as finance, marketing, healthcare, e-commerce, and consulting. Key Career Paths are:
1. Business Analyst
2. Data Analyst
3. Operations Manager
4. Market Research Analyst
5. Business Intelligence Specialist
6. Data Scientist
