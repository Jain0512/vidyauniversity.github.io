Apple Lab


The Apple Lab at vidya university is a state-of-the-art facility designed to provide students with hands-on experience in Apple’s cutting-edge ecosystem. Equipped with the latest MacBooks, iPads, and development tools, the lab serves as a hub for innovation, creativity, and technical excellence.
Key Features:
Mac Ecosystem – The lab features high-performance iMacs and MacBooks with powerful M-series processors, enabling seamless software development and design work.
iOS App Development – Students can build, test, and deploy applications using Xcode, Swift, and Objective-C, fostering expertise in iOS development.
Design & Creativity – With industry-leading tools like Final Cut Pro, Logic Pro, and Adobe Creative Cloud, students can explore UI/UX design, video editing, and music production.
Cloud & AI Integration – Access to Apple’s CloudKit, Core ML, and ARKit enables students to develop intelligent applications with cloud storage, machine learning, and augmented reality capabilities.
Hands-on Learning & Certifications – The lab offers training sessions, workshops, and certification programs such as Swift Developer Certification to help students gain industry-recognized credentials.
