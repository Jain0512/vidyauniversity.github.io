Faculty of Education & Sports


Vidya University's Faculty of Education & Sports is dedicated to nurturing future educators and sports professionals. We offer a diverse range of programs, from undergraduate to doctoral levels, that provide a strong foundation in education and sports science. Our curriculum is designed to blend theoretical knowledge with practical experience. Experienced faculty members guide students through a variety of learning methods, including lectures, seminars, workshops, and hands-on training. Beyond academics, we encourage students to participate in extracurricular activities, sports, and cultural events. These activities promote holistic development and foster essential skills like teamwork, leadership, and communication. Our graduates are well-prepared for a variety of careers in education and sports, including teaching, coaching, sports management, sports journalism, and physical therapy. By choosing Vidya University's Faculty of Education & Sports, you'll be equipped to make a positive impact on society.
