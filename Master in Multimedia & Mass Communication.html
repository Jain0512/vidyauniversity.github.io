Master in Multimedia & Mass Communication


The Master of Multimedia and Mass Communication is a postgraduate program that combines both theoretical knowledge and practical skills in the fields of media, journalism, filmmaking, public relations, and digital communication. Students are exposed to a wide range of media platforms, including television, radio, print, and online media, and are trained in content creation, editing, broadcasting, and media management. The curriculum also emphasizes critical thinking, media ethics, and the impact of media on society. With hands-on experience through internships and projects, graduates are well-equipped for careers in broadcasting, media production, digital content creation, advertising, and public relations.
