BFA (Applied Arts)


The BFA in Applied Arts program bridges artistic creativity with commercial design. Students learn graphic design, advertising, branding, and digital media techniques. The curriculum includes typography, photography, UI/UX design, and visual communication. Industry collaborations provide exposure to real-world design challenges. Practical projects help students build strong portfolios for career advancement. The program emphasizes both traditional and digital design methodologies. Guest lectures from industry experts offer insights into emerging design trends. Hands-on workshops improve technical proficiency in various design software. Graduates can work in advertising agencies, design studios, or as freelance designers. The program prepares students for diverse careers in creative industries.
