MBA PLUS (Health Care Management)


The MBA PLUS at Vidya University provides additional Processes, Learning, Up-skilling, and Specialization, making it a transformative two-year program tailored for aspiring professionals seeking to revolutionize the healthcare sector. By merging core business acumen with advanced healthcare insights, this program equips students to spearhead strategic initiatives and manage complex healthcare systems. Designed to address the dynamic challenges of the industry, the curriculum delves into healthcare operations, policy formulation, financial stewardship, marketing strategies, and strategic leadership. Graduates emerge as empowered leaders, ready to excel in hospitals, pharmaceutical enterprises, health insurance firms, and other pivotal healthcare sectors.
