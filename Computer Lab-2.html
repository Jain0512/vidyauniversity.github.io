Computer Lab-2


Computer Lab 2 is a specialized facility exclusively designed for students of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML). Equipped with high-performance computing resources, advanced AI frameworks, and cloud integration, this lab provides an ideal environment for research, development, and practical applications in AI, deep learning, and data science.

Lab Features & Infrastructure

✅ High-Performance GPUs & Workstations for deep learning and AI model training.
✅ Cloud Computing Support with access to Google Colab, AWS, Azure, and TensorFlow Cloud.
✅ Pre-installed AI & ML Software & Frameworks, including:

Machine Learning: Scikit-learn, TensorFlow, PyTorch
Deep Learning: Keras, OpenCV, NLP Libraries (spaCy, NLTK)
Big Data & Analytics: Hadoop, Spark, Pandas, NumPy
Data Visualization: Matplotlib, Seaborn, Power BI
Programming Tools: Jupyter Notebook, Anaconda, VS Code

Purpose & Usage

Hands-on AI & ML Training: Supports coursework in Neural Networks, Deep Learning, NLP, and Computer Vision.
Research & Development: Facilitates AI model development, automation, and robotics projects.
Big Data Processing: Enables students to work on large-scale data analytics and cloud-based AI models.
Workshops & Certifications: Hosts AI, ML, and Data Science bootcamps, hackathons, and industry collaborations.

Guidelines & Access

Open for AI & ML students during academic hours with extended access for research projects.
Supervised by faculty and lab assistants to ensure best practices and guidance.
Strict compliance with ethical AI practices and responsible computing usage.
