Bachelor in Multimedia & Mass Communication


The Bachelor of Multimedia and Mass Communication program at Vidya University offers a dynamic and industry-focused curriculum that prepares students to excel in the ever-evolving media landscape. The program blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on training in areas such as Journalism, Digital Media, Public Relations and Filmmaking. Students gain expertise in storytelling, content creation, media ethics and communication strategies. Graduates emerge as skilled media professionals capable of adapting to global trends. This program empowers students to influence, inform and inspire audiences across diverse platforms.
The curriculum blends graphic design fundamentals with the principles of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, ensuring that students graduate with a well-rounded understanding of how to craft seamless, aesthetically pleasing, and functional designs.

Career Opportunities


Bachelor in Multimedia and Mass Communication graduates have a wide variety of career options in the media and communication industry. They can work as journalists, content creators, or reporters in print, broadcast, or digital media. Other roles include public relations specialists, advertising professionals, video producers, graphic designers, social media managers, or media planners. Graduates can also work in the film and television industry as editors, directors, or production assistants. With the growing demand for digital content, opportunities in online media, digital marketing, and broadcasting are also available, making this degree ideal for those with a passion for storytelling and media production.
