Ph.D in Journalism & Mass Communication


The Bachelor of Multimedia and Mass Communication program at Vidya University offers a dynamic and industry-focused curriculum that prepares students to excel in the ever-evolving media landscape. The program blends theoretical knowledge with hands-on training in areas such as Journalism, Digital Media, Public Relations and Filmmaking. Students gain expertise in storytelling, content creation, media ethics and communication strategies. Graduates emerge as skilled media professionals capable of adapting to global trends. This program empowers students to influence, inform and inspire audiences across diverse platforms. The curriculum blends graphic design fundamentals with the principles of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, ensuring that students graduate with a well-rounded understanding of how to craft seamless, aesthetically pleasing, and functional designs.
