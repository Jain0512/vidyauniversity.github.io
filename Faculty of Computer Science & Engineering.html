Faculty of Computer Science & Engineering


Vidya University faculty of Computer Science and Engineering is dedicated to producing top-notch tech professionals. With experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and an industry-focused curriculum, the department aims to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the competitive tech industry. We offer a blend of theoretical and practical learning, with a strong emphasis on hands-on projects and real-world applications. Students have access to cutting-edge technology and software, allowing them to explore emerging trends and innovate. The department also encourages research and innovation, fostering a culture of creativity and problem-solving. Faculty members actively engage in research, and students are encouraged to participate in coding competitions, hackathons, and other extracurricular activities.
