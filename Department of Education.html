Department of Education


The Department of Education, under the Faculty of Sports & Education, is dedicated to shaping future educators, trainers, and sports professionals. The department integrates pedagogical theories, practical teaching methodologies, and sports education to develop well-rounded individuals who can contribute to the education and sports sectors effectively. With a strong focus on innovative teaching techniques, psychological aspects of learning, and sports science, the department aims to produce skilled educators and sports professionals who can inspire and lead in academic and athletic fields.
