Department of Electronics Engineering


The Department of Electronics Engineering is dedicated to advancing technology through innovation, research, and practical applications. Our goal is to equip students with a strong foundation in electronics, embedded systems, communication technology, VLSI design, and IoT. With a blend of theoretical knowledge and hands-on training, we prepare students for careers in diverse fields such as semiconductors, telecommunications, robotics, and embedded systems.

Programs Offered :

We offer industry-relevant programs that focus on modern electronics and communication technologies:

  • B.Tech in Electronics Engineering

  • M.Tech in Electronics & Communication
