Ph.D (Fine Arts)


The Ph.D. in Fine Arts is a research-intensive program designed for individuals seeking to achieve academic and creative excellence in the field of Fine Arts. This program blends theoretical exploration with advanced creative practice, encouraging innovative contributions to art and culture. Students can pursue research in areas such as art history, contemporary practices, aesthetics, multimedia art, and more, under the mentorship of distinguished faculty. With a focus on interdisciplinary learning and critical inquiry, the program prepares graduates for leadership roles in academia, museums, cultural institutions, and creative industries.
