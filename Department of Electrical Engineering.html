Department of Electrical Engineering


The Department of Electrical Engineering is committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation in electrical systems, power engineering, and emerging technologies. Our department prepares students with strong analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills to excel in industries such as power generation, renewable energy, automation, and embedded systems. With a curriculum that integrates theoretical concepts and hands-on experience, students gain expertise in power electronics, control systems, electrical machines, and smart grids.
