B.Com. in Banking & Insurance


Our B. Com in Banking and Insurance, a three-year undergraduate degree, is designed to prepare you for a thriving career in these dynamic industries. This program provides the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the ever-evolving fields of Banking and Insurance.
In collaboration with the Insurance Institute of India, the program includes specialized subjects from the institute in each semester, adding valuable industry-relevant credentials to your academic journey.
Throughout the program, you will build a strong foundation in core commerce disciplines, such as accounting, finance, and economics. Additionally, the curriculum offers specialized knowledge in key areas, including:
1. Banking Operations and Management
2. Risk Management and Financial Analysis
3. Insurance Principles and Practices
4. Investment Banking and Wealth Management
5. Financial Technology (FinTech)
