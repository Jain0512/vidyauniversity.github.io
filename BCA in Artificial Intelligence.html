BCA in Artificial Intelligence


The Artificial Intelligence program at Vidya University offers a comprehensive and industry-relevant curriculum designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the world of AI and ML. The program blends theoretical foundations with hands-on experience, covering key areas such as machine learning algorithms, deep learning, natural language processing, computer vision, and data analytics. With a strong emphasis on practical learning, students engage in projects, internships, and real-world applications, preparing them to solve complex problems using AI-driven technologies.
